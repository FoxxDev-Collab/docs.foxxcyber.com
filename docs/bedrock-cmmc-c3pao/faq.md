# Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about using the Bedrock CMMC C3PAO portal.

## Engagements

### How do I receive assessment requests?

Organizations find your C3PAO through the marketplace and submit assessment requests. Requests appear in your **Engagements** section with "Requested" status. You'll receive email notifications for new requests.

### Can I accept multiple engagements simultaneously?

Yes, you can manage multiple concurrent engagements. Consider your team capacity and assessor availability when accepting new requests.

### How do I decline a request?

Click on the engagement, then select **Decline**. You'll be prompted to provide a reason. The organization will be notified and can request another C3PAO.

### What if a customer cancels mid-assessment?

Either party can cancel an engagement. Navigate to the engagement and select **Cancel**. Document the reason for your records.

## Assessment Process

### How long should an assessment take?

Assessment duration varies based on:
- Organization size and complexity
- CMMC level being assessed
- Responsiveness of the organization
- Number of systems in scope

Typical assessments range from 4-12 weeks from acceptance to results submission.

### Can I request additional evidence during assessment?

Yes, use the **Request Evidence** feature on any control. The organization receives notification and can upload additional artifacts.

### How do I handle conflicting evidence?

Document the conflict in your control notes. Request clarification from the organization. Make your determination based on the preponderance of evidence.

### What if I disagree with a team member's determination?

Use the internal notes feature to discuss the control. The lead assessor makes final determination. Quality review can also address inconsistencies.

## Team Management

### How do I add assessors to my organization?

Navigate to **Settings > Team > Add Member**. Enter their email and role. They'll receive an invitation to join.

### Can assessors be assigned to specific domains?

Yes, when adding assessors to an engagement, you can assign them to specific CMMC domains or individual controls.

### What credentials are required?

All assessors must hold valid CCA certification. Lead assessors need the Lead Assessor designation. The platform tracks credential status and expiration.

## Platform Usage

### What browsers are supported?

Bedrock CMMC supports:
- Google Chrome (recommended)
- Microsoft Edge
- Mozilla Firefox
- Apple Safari

### Can I access the platform on mobile devices?

The platform is designed for desktop use. Some features may work on tablets, but full functionality requires a desktop browser.

### How do I export assessment data?

Navigate to **Reports** within an engagement. Select the desired report type and format (PDF, Excel, or JSON). OSCAL export is also available.

### Is customer data secure?

Yes, the platform implements:
- Encryption in transit and at rest
- Role-based access controls
- Audit logging of all actions
- SOC 2 Type II certified infrastructure

## Results and Certification

### What determines Pass, Conditional, or Fail?

- **Pass**: All controls Met or N/A with justification
- **Conditional**: Minor findings documented in acceptable POA&Ms
- **Fail**: Critical gaps or numerous Not Met determinations

### Can I change results after submission?

Results are locked after submission. Contact your C3PAO administrator if corrections are needed. An amendment process is available for errors.

### Who sees the assessment results?

Results are shared with:
- The assessed organization
- Your C3PAO assessment team
- CMMC-AB (for certification processing)

## Marketplace

### How do I improve my marketplace visibility?

- Complete your organization profile fully
- Upload a professional logo
- Maintain current availability status
- Respond to requests promptly
- Encourage customer reviews

### Can I set my own pricing?

Pricing is handled outside the platform. The marketplace facilitates connections but doesn't process payments.

### How do customer reviews work?

After assessment completion, organizations can leave reviews. Reviews include a star rating and written feedback. You can respond to reviews professionally.

## Technical Issues

### I can't access an engagement. What do I do?

Verify you're assigned to the engagement. Check with your organization administrator about permissions. Contact support if issues persist.

### Evidence files won't download. What's wrong?

Check your browser's popup blocker. Try a different browser. Verify your internet connection. Large files may take time to download.

### How do I report a bug or issue?

Contact support at support@bedrockcmmc.com or visit [foxxcyber.com/support](https://foxxcyber.com/support).
