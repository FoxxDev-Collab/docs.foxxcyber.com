## Platform Overview

The Bedrock CMMC Platform enables C3PAO organizations to:

- Receive and manage assessment requests from the customer marketplace
- Review complete ATO packages including SSP, evidence, STIGs, and POA&Ms
- Assess all 110 NIST SP 800-171 requirements with structured findings
- Generate assessment reports with executive summaries and recommendations
- Export assessment results directly to eMASS format
- Manage your team's workload and engagement assignments
- Maintain your organization's marketplace profile and credentials
