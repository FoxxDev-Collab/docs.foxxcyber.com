## Join the Beta Program

We're inviting organizations to participate in our public beta program. As a beta participant, you'll get:

- **Early access** to Bedrock CMMC before general availability
- **Direct influence** on product development through feedback
- **Priority support** from our team
- **Special pricing** for early adopters

## Who Should Join

The beta is ideal for:

- Defense Industrial Base (DIB) contractors preparing for CMMC certification
- Managed Security Service Providers (MSSPs) supporting DIB clients
- Organizations currently working toward NIST SP 800-171 compliance
- C3PAOs interested in streamlined assessment workflows

## What to Expect

During the beta period, we'll be:

- Gathering feedback on core features
- Testing at scale with real compliance workflows
- Refining the user experience
- Hardening security and performance
